Coffee Circle

Boosting Creativity for Innovation, HEC Paris

Prompt1: Design an innovative coffee house

  1. Has 5 divisions depending on purpose: Party mood, study mood, professional meeting mood, relax mood, family gathering mood.
  2. Table are in form of triangles since if there are multiple people traingles can always combine to give a bigger shape
  3. In each section music and the entire atmosphere is according to the purpose and predecided mood
  4. At the back of each the coffee brewing counters are located, where a person and walk and order

Prompt2: Make a modification to an existing product at the coffee house

We usually see the food menu where we order the coffee. In my suggestion, food will be placed under the tables within a glass case and there will be a vending machine-like mechanism to order the food you want. A heating device placed on the table so you can heat your food.

  • Relocate the food items to a tray under the table
  • Convenient for the customer, he won’t have to call the waiter
  • Convenient for coffee house owner since seeing food in front of them will tempt customers to order food and hence could lead to increase in revenue
  • There can be a system to track the food left in the glass case, so more food can be prepared and added in the machine
  • No hassle of money exchange since ease of using a vending machine.
